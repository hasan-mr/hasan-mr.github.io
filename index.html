I am an Assistant Professor of Practice in the School of Computing at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln.

I am enthusiastic about exploring the frontier of Machine Learning and Deep Learning. I create intelligent algorithms to discover patterns in the Hamletian "to be or not to be" realm of Big Data! More specifically, I am interested to develop algorithms for learning generalizable representations of data that improves both effectiveness and efficiency of ML/DL models.
